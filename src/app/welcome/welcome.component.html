About

Digible Master is an unoffical fangame made on top of Digible.io.
All seen NTF collectibles named "Digibles" are fetched from the Blockchain (Currently TESTNET).

Buy and collect these wonderful digibles and be their owner.
Become the master of digibles by fighting other collectors world wide!
